Health:

This stat indicates your overall health and well-being. It decreases when you take damage and can be increased by healing items or resting.

Stamina:

Stamina represents your energy levels for physical activities. It depletes during intense actions and regenerates over time.

Focus:

Focus is essential for performing tasks effectively. A higher focus improves your performance in critical situations.

A:

This stat represents a special attribute affecting various gameplay aspects. Enhancing it can provide strategic advantages.

B:

This stat impacts your overall capabilities in the game. Balancing this with other attributes is key to success.
